As part of my role as a Post-16 Outreach Officer at the University of East Anglia (UEA), I lead engagement with parents, carers, teachers, and advisers who support students considering Higher Education.

To help these supporters navigate one of the busiest stages of the application cycle, I led the development of this comprehensive guide to UCAS Clearing. The resource explains what Clearing is, who it is for, and how the process works through a step-by-step walkthrough, complete with screenshots of the student-facing system, practical advice for contacting universities, and answers to a wide range of frequently asked questions.

Although I led the project from conception to publication, it was developed in collaboration with colleagues from student recruitment, access and participation outreach, admissions, marketing, and our external creative partner, [Mammoth](https://www.mmmth.co.uk/).

[Download the guide here](/files/2026-03-16-uea-clearing-guide.pdf), or [read the press release](https://www.uea.ac.uk/about/news/article/free-resources-to-support-students-preparing-for-a-level-results-day-and-clearing), which highlights this guide alongside the wider range of support and resources offered by UEA for Clearing 2026.
